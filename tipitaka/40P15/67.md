# 67 Niyyānikaduka, Kusalattika

(76.)

205\. Niyyānikaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naniyyāniko nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Aniyyānikaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naniyyāniko nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā dve…pe…  avigate dve.

206\. Aniyyānikaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naniyyāniko naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ. (Abyākatamūlaṃ dve paccayavasena.)
