# 32 Āsavaduka, Kusalattika

(41.)

119\. Noāsavaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca noāsavo nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ…pe…  avigate ekaṃ.

121\. Noāsavaṃ abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paccayā nanoāsavo naabyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
