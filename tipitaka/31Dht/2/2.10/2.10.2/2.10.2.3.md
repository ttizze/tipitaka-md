

# 2.10.2.3 Āsavagocchaka





(392.)

482\. Āsavehi dhammehi ye dhammā…  āsavasampayuttehi dhammehi ye dhammā…  āsavehi ceva sāsavehi ca dhammehi ye dhammā…  āsavehi ceva āsavasampayuttehi ca dhammehi ye dhammā…  āsavasampayuttehi ceva no ca āsavehi dhammehi ye dhammā vippayuttā, tehi dhammehi ye dhammā vippayuttā…  te dhammā ekena khandhena dasahāyatanehi soḷasahi dhātūhi vippayuttā; ekenāyatanena ekāya dhātuyā kehici vippayuttā.

(393.)

483\. Anāsavehi dhammehi ye dhammā…  āsavavippayuttehi anāsavehi dhammehi ye dhammā vippayuttā, tehi dhammehi ye dhammā vippayuttā…  te dhammā na kehici khandhehi na kehici āyatanehi chahi dhātūhi vippayuttā.
