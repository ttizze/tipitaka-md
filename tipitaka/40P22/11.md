

# 11 Kusalattika, Arūpāvacaraduka


(31.)

71\. Nakusalaṃ naarūpāvacaraṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo arūpāvacaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha…pe…  avigate cha.

72\. Nakusalaṃ nanaarūpāvacaraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato naarūpāvacaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  avigate pañca.
