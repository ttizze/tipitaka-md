

# 13 Kusalattika, Hīnattika


(17.)

60\. Nakusalaṃ nahīnaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā akusalo hīno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

61\. Nakusalaṃ namajjhimaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato majjhimo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.

62\. Nakusalaṃ napaṇītaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo paṇīto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha…pe…  āsevane tīṇi…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
