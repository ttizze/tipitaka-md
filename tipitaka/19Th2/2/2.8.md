

# 2.8 Abhayamātutherīgāthā



(33.)

60\. _“Uddhaṃ pādatalā amma,_  
_adho ve kesamatthakā;_  
_Paccavekkhassumaṃ kāyaṃ,_  
_asuciṃ pūtigandhikaṃ._

_sabbo rāgo samūhato;_  
_Pariḷāho samucchinno,_  
_sītibhūtāmhi nibbutā”ti._
