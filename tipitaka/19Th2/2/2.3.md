

# 2.3 Sumaṅgalamātātherīgāthā



(23.)

45\. _“Sumuttikā sumuttikā,_  
_Sādhumuttikāmhi musalassa;_  
_Ahiriko me chattakaṃ vāpi,_  
_Ukkhalikā me deḍḍubhaṃ vāti._  


(24.)

46\. _Rāgañca ahaṃ dosañca,_  
_Cicciṭi cicciṭīti vihanāmi;_  
_Sā rukkhamūlamupagamma,_  
_Aho sukhanti sukhato jhāyāmī”ti._  


---

47\. …  Sumaṅgalamātā therī.
