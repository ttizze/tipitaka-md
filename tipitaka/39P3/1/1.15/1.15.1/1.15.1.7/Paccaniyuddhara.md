# Paccanīyuddhāra

(435.)

944\. Hetu micchattaniyato dhammo hetussa micchattaniyatassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

(436.)

945\. Nahetuyā nava, naārammaṇe nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

946\. Hetupaccayā naadhipatiyā tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

947\. Nahetupaccayā adhipatiyā tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

948\. (Yathā kusalattike pañhāvārassa anulomampi paccanīyampi anulomapaccanīyampi paccanīyānulomampi gaṇitaṃ, evaṃ gaṇetabbaṃ.)
