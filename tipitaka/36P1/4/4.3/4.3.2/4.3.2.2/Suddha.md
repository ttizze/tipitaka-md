# Suddha

(78.)

2236\. Nahetuyā dvādasa, naārammaṇe pañca, naadhipatiyā sattarasa, naanantare pañca, nasamanantare pañca, naaññamaññe pañca, naupanissaye pañca, napurejāte dvādasa, napacchājāte sattarasa, naāsevane sattarasa, nakamme cattāri, navipāke nava, naāhāre ekaṃ, naindriye ekaṃ, najhāne cattāri, namagge nava, nasampayutte pañca, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā pañca, novigate pañca.
