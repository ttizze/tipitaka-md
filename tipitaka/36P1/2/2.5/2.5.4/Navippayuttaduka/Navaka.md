# Navaka

816\. Navippayuttapaccayā nahetupaccayā naadhipatipaccayā napurejātapaccayā napacchājātapaccayā (naāsevanapaccayamūlakampi nahetumūlakasadisaṃ.) Nakammapaccayā navipākapaccayā namaggapaccayā (imāni tīṇi mūlāni ekasadisāni) ārammaṇe ekaṃ, anantare ekaṃ, samanantare ekaṃ, sahajāte ekaṃ, aññamaññe ekaṃ, nissaye ekaṃ, upanissaye ekaṃ, āhāre ekaṃ, indriye ekaṃ, jhāne ekaṃ, sampayutte ekaṃ, atthiyā ekaṃ, natthiyā ekaṃ, vigate ekaṃ, avigate ekaṃ.

---

817\. Paccanīyānulomaṃ.

Saṃsaṭṭhavāro.
