# Cittādhipatighaṭanā

(424.)

1316\. Adhipati sahajāta nissaya āhāra indriya atthi avigatanti nahetuyā satta, naārammaṇe satta, naanantare satta, nasamanantare satta, naaññamaññe tīṇi, naupanissaye satta, napurejāte satta, napacchājāte satta, naāsevane satta, nakamme satta, navipāke satta, najhāne satta, namagge satta, nasampayutte tīṇi, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā satta, novigate satta.
