# Aṭṭhaka

1724\. Naaññamaññapaccayā nahetupaccayā naārammaṇapaccayā naadhipatipaccayā naanantarapaccayā nasamanantarapaccayā nasahajātapaccayā nissaye tīṇi, upanissaye nava, purejāte tīṇi, pacchājāte tīṇi, kamme dve, āhāre ekaṃ, indriye ekaṃ, vippayutte pañca, atthiyā satta, avigate satta. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

---

1725\. Naaññamaññamūlakaṃ.
