

# Paccanīyuddhāra




(391.)

1158\. Kusalo dhammo kusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo.

1159\. Kusalo dhammo akusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo.

1160\. Kusalo dhammo abyākatassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo.

1161\. Kusalo dhammo kusalassa ca abyākatassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (4)

(392.)

1162\. Akusalo dhammo akusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo.

1163\. Akusalo dhammo kusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo.

1164\. Akusalo dhammo abyākatassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo.

1165\. Akusalo dhammo akusalassa ca abyākatassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (4)

(393.)

1166\. Abyākato dhammo abyākatassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo.

1167\. Abyākato dhammo kusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo.

1168\. Abyākato dhammo akusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo. (3)

(394.)

1169\. Kusalo ca abyākato ca dhammā kusalassa dhammassa sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ.

1170\. Kusalo ca abyākato ca dhammā abyākatassa dhammassa sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. (2)

(395.)

1171\. Akusalo ca abyākato ca dhammā akusalassa dhammassa sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ.

1172\. Akusalo ca abyākato ca dhammā abyākatassa dhammassa sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. (2)
