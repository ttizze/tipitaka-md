

# Dasaka







1223\. Napacchājātapaccayā nahetupaccayā naārammaṇapaccayā naadhipatipaccayā naanantarapaccayā nasamanantarapaccayā nasahajātapaccayā naaññamaññapaccayā nanissayapaccayā naupanissaye tīṇi, napurejāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme nava, navipāke nava, naāhāre nava, naindriye nava, najhāne nava, namagge nava, nasampayutte nava, navippayutte nava, noatthiyā nava, nonatthiyā nava, novigate nava, noavigate nava.
