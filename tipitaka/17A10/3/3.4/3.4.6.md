

# 3.4.6 Sāsavasutta




(139.)

1116\. “Sāsavañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṃ desessāmi anāsavañca. Taṃ suṇātha…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, sāsavo dhammo? Micchādiṭṭhi…pe…  micchāvimutti—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sāsavo dhammo. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, anāsavo dhammo? Sammādiṭṭhi…pe…  sammāvimutti—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, anāsavo dhammo”ti.
