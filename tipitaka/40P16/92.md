# 92 Sanidassanattika, Niyyānikaduka

(143.)

340\. Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ niyyānikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho naniyyāniko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  avigate cha.

341\. Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ aniyyānikaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā nasanidassanasappaṭigho naaniyyāniko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
