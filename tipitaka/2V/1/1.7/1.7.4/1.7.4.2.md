

# 1.7.4.2 Pattasaññīsikkhāpada





(607.)

1840\. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū tahaṃ tahaṃ olokentā piṇḍapātaṃ bhuñjanti, ākirantepi atikkantepi na jānanti…pe… .

1841\. **“Pattasaññī piṇḍapātaṃ bhuñjissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”**ti. (*32*:**177**)

1842\. Pattasaññinā piṇḍapāto bhuñjitabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca tahaṃ tahaṃ olokento piṇḍapātaṃ bhuñjati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

1843\. Anāpatti— asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.

---

1844\. Dutiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
