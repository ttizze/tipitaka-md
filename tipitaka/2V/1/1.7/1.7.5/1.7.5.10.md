# 1.7.5.10 Capucapukārakasikkhāpada

(626.)

1934\. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū capucapukārakaṃ bhuñjanti…pe… .

1935\. **“Na capucapukārakaṃ bhuñjissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”**ti. (*50*:**195**)

1936\. Na capucapukārakaṃ bhuñjitabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca capucapukārakaṃ bhuñjati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

1937\. Anāpatti— asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.

---

1938\. Dasamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

Kabaḷavaggo pañcamo.
