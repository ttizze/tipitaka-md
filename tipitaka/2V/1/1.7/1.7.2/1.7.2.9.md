# 1.7.2.9 Sīsappacālakasikkhāpada

(594.)

1775\. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū sīsappacālakaṃ antaraghare gacchanti sīsaṃ olambentā…pe… .

1776\. **“Na sīsappacālakaṃ antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”**ti. (*19*:**164**)

1777\. Na sīsappacālakaṃ antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Sīsaṃ paggahetvā gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca sīsappacālakaṃ antaraghare gacchati sīsaṃ olambento, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

1778\. Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.

---

1779\. Navamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
