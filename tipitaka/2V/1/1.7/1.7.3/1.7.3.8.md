# 1.7.3.8 Pattasaññīpaṭiggahaṇasikkhāpada

(603.)

1820\. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū tahaṃ tahaṃ olokentā piṇḍapātaṃ paṭiggaṇhanti, ākirantepi atikkantepi na jānanti…pe… .

1821\. **“Pattasaññī piṇḍapātaṃ paṭiggahessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”**ti. (*28*:**173**)

1822\. Pattasaññinā piṇḍapāto paṭiggahetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca tahaṃ tahaṃ olokento piṇḍapātaṃ paṭiggaṇhāti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

1823\. Anāpatti— asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.

---

1824\. Aṭṭhamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
