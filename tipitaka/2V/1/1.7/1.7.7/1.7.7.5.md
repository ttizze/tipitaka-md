

# 1.7.7.5 Pallatthikasikkhāpada





(642.)

2023\. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū pallatthikāya nisinnassa dhammaṃ desenti…pe… .

2024\. **“Na pallatthikāya nisinnassa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”**ti. (*65*:**210**)

2025\. Na pallatthikāya nisinnassa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca hatthapallatthikāya vā dussapallatthikāya vā nisinnassa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

2026\. Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.

---

2027\. Pañcamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
