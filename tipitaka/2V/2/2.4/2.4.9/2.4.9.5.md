

# 2.4.9.5 Gandhavaṇṇakasikkhāpada





(1198.)

3469\. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyanti. Manussā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyissanti, seyyathāpi gihiniyo kāmabhoginiyo”ti.

3470\. Assosuṃ kho bhikkhuniyo tesaṃ manussānaṃ ujjhāyantānaṃ khiyyantānaṃ vipācentānaṃ. Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā…pe…  tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyissantī”ti…pe…  “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyantī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā…pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyissanti. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya…pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—

(1199.)

3471\. **“Yā pana bhikkhunī gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyeyya, pācittiyan”**ti. (*88*:**143**)

(1200.)

3472\. **Yā panā**ti yā yādisā…pe…  **bhikkhunī**ti…pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.

3473\. **Gandho** nāma yo koci gandho. **Vaṇṇakaṃ** nāma yaṃ kiñci vaṇṇakaṃ. **Nahāyeyyā**ti nahāyati. Payoge dukkaṭaṃ, nahānapariyosāne āpatti pācittiyassa.

(1201.)

3474\. Anāpatti—  ābādhappaccayā, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

---

3475\. Pañcamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
