# 4.10 Sāriputtaupasamasutta

(40.)

343\. Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena āyasmā sāriputto bhagavato avidūre nisinno hoti pallaṅkaṃ ābhujitvā ujuṃ kāyaṃ paṇidhāya attano upasamaṃ paccavekkhamāno.

344\. Addasā kho bhagavā āyasmantaṃ sāriputtaṃ avidūre nisinnaṃ pallaṅkaṃ ābhujitvā ujuṃ kāyaṃ paṇidhāya attano upasamaṃ paccavekkhamānaṃ.

345\. Atha kho bhagavā etamatthaṃ viditvā tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imaṃ udānaṃ udānesi—

346\. _“Upasantasantacittassa,_  
_netticchinnassa bhikkhuno;_  
_Vikkhīṇo jātisaṃsāro,_  
_mutto so mārabandhanā”ti. (50)_

347\. Dasamaṃ.

---

348\. Meghiyavaggo catuttho.

349\. Tassuddānaṃ

350\. _Meghiyo uddhatā gopālo,_  
_yakkho nāgena pañcamaṃ;_  
_Piṇḍolo sāriputto ca,_  
_sundarī bhavati aṭṭhamaṃ;_  
_Upaseno vaṅgantaputto,_  
_sāriputto ca te dasāti._
