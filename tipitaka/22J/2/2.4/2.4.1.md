

# 2.4.1 Asadisajātaka




(61.)

438\. _Dhanuggaho asadiso,_  
_rājaputto mahabbalo;_  
_Dūrepātī akkhaṇavedhī,_  
_mahākāyappadālano._  


(62.)

439\. _Sabbāmitte raṇaṃ katvā,_  
_na ca kañci viheṭhayi;_  
_Bhātaraṃ sotthiṃ katvāna,_  
_saṃyamaṃ ajjhupāgamīti._
