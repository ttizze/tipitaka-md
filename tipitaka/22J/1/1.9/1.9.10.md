# 1.9.10 Akataññujātaka

(90.)

198\. _“Yo pubbe katakalyāṇo,_  
_katattho nāvabujjhati;_  
_Pacchā kicce samuppanne,_  
_kattāraṃ nādhigacchatī”ti._  

---

199\. Akataññujātakaṃ dasamaṃ.

Apāyimhavaggo navamo.

---

200\. Tassuddānaṃ

201\. _Apāyimha ca dūbhaka sattapadaṃ,_  
_Chaḷadvara ca āyatinā ca puna;_  
_Ahisīlava maṅgali pāpikassā,_  
_Sataṃnikkha katatthavarena dasāti._
