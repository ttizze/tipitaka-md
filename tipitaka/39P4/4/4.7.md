# 4.7 Kusalattika, Saññojanadukādi

(34.)

1329\. Akusalaṃ saññojanaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  akusalaṃ ganthaṃ…  oghaṃ…  yogaṃ…  nīvaraṇaṃ… .
