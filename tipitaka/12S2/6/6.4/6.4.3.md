# 6.4.3 Kusaladhammasutta

(182.)

1088\. Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. “Dāruṇo, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko. Lābhasakkārasilokena abhibhūtassa pariyādiṇṇacittassa, bhikkhave, devadattassa kusalo dhammo samucchedamagamā. Evaṃ dāruṇo kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko…pe…  sikkhitabban”ti.

---

1089\. Tatiyaṃ.
