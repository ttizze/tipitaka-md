# 8.1.5 Asilomasutta

(206.)

1169\. “Idhāhaṃ, āvuso, gijjhakūṭā pabbatā orohanto addasaṃ asilomaṃ purisaṃ vehāsaṃ gacchantaṃ. Tassa te asī uppatitvā uppatitvā tasseva kāye nipatanti. So sudaṃ aṭṭassaraṃ karoti…pe…  eso, bhikkhave, satto imasmiṃyeva rājagahe sūkariko ahosi…pe… .
