# 8.1.6 Sattisutta

(207.)

1171\. “Idhāhaṃ, āvuso, gijjhakūṭā pabbatā orohanto addasaṃ sattilomaṃ purisaṃ vehāsaṃ gacchantaṃ. Tassa tā sattiyo uppatitvā uppatitvā tasseva kāye nipatanti. So sudaṃ aṭṭassaraṃ karoti…pe…  eso, bhikkhave, satto imasmiṃyeva rājagahe māgaviko ahosi…pe… .

1172\. Chaṭṭhaṃ.
