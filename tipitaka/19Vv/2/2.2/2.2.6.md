# 2.2.6 Bhikkhādāyakavimānavatthu

(1081.)

927\. _“Uccamidaṃ maṇithūṇaṃ vimānaṃ,_  
_Samantato dvādasa yojanāni;_  
_Kūṭāgārā sattasatā uḷārā,_  
_Veḷuriyathambhā rucakatthatā subhā._  

(1082.)

928\. _Deviddhipattosi mahānubhāvo,_  
_Manussabhūto kimakāsi puññaṃ;_  
_Kenāsi evaṃ jalitānubhāvo,_  
_Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti._  

(1083.)

929\. _So devaputto attamano,_  
_…pe…_  
_yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

(1084.)

930\. _“Ahaṃ manussesu manussabhūto,_  
_Disvāna bhikkhuṃ tasitaṃ kilantaṃ;_  
_Ekāhaṃ bhikkhaṃ paṭipādayissaṃ,_  
_Samaṅgi bhattena tadā akāsiṃ._  

(1085--1086.)

931\. _Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,_  
_…pe…_  
_Vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti._  

---

932\. Bhikkhādāyakavimānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
