

# 2.3.5 Ambavimānavatthu




(1146.)

995\. _“Uccamidaṃ maṇithūṇaṃ vimānaṃ,_  
_Samantato dvādasa yojanāni;_  
_Kūṭāgārā sattasatā uḷārā,_  
_Veḷuriyathambhā rucakatthatā subhā._  


(1147.)

996\. _Tatthacchasi pivasi khādasi ca,_  
_Dibbā ca vīṇā pavadanti vagguṃ;_  
_Dibbā rasā kāmaguṇettha pañca,_  
_Nāriyo ca naccanti suvaṇṇachannā._  


(1148--1149.)

997\. _Kena tetādiso vaṇṇo,_  
_…pe…_  
_Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti._  


(1150.)

998\. _So devaputto attamano,_  
_…pe…_  
_yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(1151.)

999\. _“Gimhānaṃ pacchime māse,_  
_patapante divaṅkare;_  
_Paresaṃ bhatako poso,_  
_ambārāmamasiñcati._  


(1152.)

1000\. _Atha tenāgamā bhikkhu,_  
_sāriputtoti vissuto;_  
_Kilantarūpo kāyena,_  
_akilantova cetasā._  


(1153.)

1001\. _Tañca disvāna āyantaṃ,_  
_Avocaṃ ambasiñcako;_  
_Sādhu taṃ bhante nhāpeyyaṃ,_  
_Yaṃ mamassa sukhāvahaṃ._  


(1154.)

1002\. _Tassa me anukampāya,_  
_nikkhipi pattacīvaraṃ;_  
_Nisīdi rukkhamūlasmiṃ,_  
_chāyāya ekacīvaro._  


(1155.)

1003\. _Tañca acchena vārinā,_  
_pasannamānaso naro;_  
_Nhāpayī rukkhamūlasmiṃ,_  
_chāyāya ekacīvaraṃ._  


(1156.)

1004\. _Ambo ca sitto samaṇo ca nhāpito,_  
_Mayā ca puññaṃ pasutaṃ anappakaṃ;_  
_Iti so pītiyā kāyaṃ,_  
_Sabbaṃ pharati attano._  


(1157.)

1005\. _Tadeva ettakaṃ kammaṃ,_  
_akāsiṃ tāya jātiyā;_  
_Pahāya mānusaṃ dehaṃ,_  
_upapannomhi nandanaṃ._  


(1158.)

1006\. _Nandane ca vane ramme,_  
_nānādijagaṇāyute;_  
_Ramāmi naccagītehi,_  
_accharāhi purakkhato”ti._  


---

1007\. Ambavimānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
