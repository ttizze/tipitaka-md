

# Navippayuttādi







(430.)

3407\. Upādinnaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca upādinno dhammo uppajjati navippayuttapaccayā—  arūpe upādinnaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  asaññasattānaṃ ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ paṭicca…pe… .

3408\. Anupādinnaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anupādinno dhammo uppajjati navippayuttapaccayā—  arūpe anupādinnaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  bāhiraṃ…  āhārasamuṭṭhānaṃ…  utusamuṭṭhānaṃ…pe…  nonatthipaccayā…  novigatapaccayā.
