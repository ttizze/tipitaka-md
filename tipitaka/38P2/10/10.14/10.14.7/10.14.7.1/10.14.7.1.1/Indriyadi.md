

# Indriyādi







(461.)

3491\. Upādinno dhammo upādinnassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  upādinnā indriyā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)

3492\. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe upādinnā indriyā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ kaṭattā ca rūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo; cakkhundriyaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa…pe…  kāyindriyaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa…pe… . **Rūpajīvitindriyaṃ** kaṭattārūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo. (2)

3493\. Upādinno dhammo anupādinnassa dhammassa…pe… . (Upādinnamūlake tīṇi, paṭhamasseva rūpajīvitindriyaṃ, itaresu natthi.) (3)

3494\. Anupādinno dhammo anupādinnassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  anupādinnā indriyā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)

3495\. Upādinno dhammo upādinnassa dhammassa jhānapaccayena paccayo…  cattāri, maggapaccayena paccayo…  cattāri, sampayuttapaccayena paccayo…  dve.
