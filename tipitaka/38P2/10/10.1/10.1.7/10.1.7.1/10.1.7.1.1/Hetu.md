

# Hetu







(27.)

2375\. Sārammaṇo dhammo sārammaṇassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo—  sārammaṇā hetū sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ hetupaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)

2376\. Sārammaṇo dhammo anārammaṇassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo—  sārammaṇā hetū cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ hetupaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (2)

2377\. Sārammaṇo dhammo sārammaṇassa ca anārammaṇassa ca dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo—  sārammaṇā hetū sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ hetupaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (3)
