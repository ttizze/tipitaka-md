# Suddha

(176.)

4463\. Hetuyā cattāri, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā nava, anantare nava, samanantare nava, sahajāte nava, aññamaññe nava, nissaye nava, upanissaye nava, purejāte tīṇi, pacchājāte tīṇi, āsevane nava, kamme cha, vipāke nava, āhāre cattāri, indriye nava, jhāne nava, magge cattāri, sampayutte cha, vippayutte pañca, atthiyā nava, natthiyā nava, vigate nava, avigate nava.

4464\. (Evaṃ paccanīyavibhaṅgopi gaṇanāpi sappītikadukasadisaṃ kātabbaṃ, yadipi vimati atthi anulomaṃ passitvā gaṇetabbaṃ.)
