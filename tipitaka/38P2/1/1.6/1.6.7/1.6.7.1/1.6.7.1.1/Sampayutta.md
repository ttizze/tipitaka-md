# Sampayutta

(191.)

517\. Nahetu sahetuko dhammo nahetusahetukassa dhammassa sampayuttapaccayena paccayo—  nahetu sahetuko eko khandho tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)
