# Nissayādi

(60.)

162\. Sahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sahetuko dhammo uppajjati nissayapaccayā…  upanissayapaccayā…  purejātapaccayā…  āsevanapaccayā…  kammapaccayā…  vipākapaccayā…  āhārapaccayā…  indriyapaccayā…  jhānapaccayā…  maggapaccayā…  (jhānampi maggampi sahajātapaccayasadisā, bāhirā mahābhūtā natthi) sampayuttapaccayā…  vippayuttapaccayā…  atthipaccayā…  natthipaccayā…  vigatapaccayā…  avigatapaccayā.
