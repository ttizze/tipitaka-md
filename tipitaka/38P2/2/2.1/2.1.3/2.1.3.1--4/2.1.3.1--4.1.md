# 2.1.3.1--4.1 Vibhaṅgavāra

(7.)

557\. Sappaccayaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā sappaccayo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  sappaccayaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paccayā tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṃ…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  khandhe paccayā vatthu, vatthuṃ paccayā khandhā, ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ…pe…  vatthuṃ paccayā sappaccayā khandhā.

558\. Sappaccayaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā sappaccayo dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
