# Kammādi

(110.)

3792\. Upādānasampayutto ceva no ca upādāno dhammo upādānasampayuttassa ceva no ca upādānassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi…  indriyapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi…  jhānapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi…  maggapaccayena paccayo…  nava…  sampayuttapaccayena paccayo…  nava…  atthipaccayena paccayo…  nava…  natthipaccayena paccayo…  nava…  vigatapaccayena paccayo…  nava…  avigatapaccayena paccayo…  nava.
