# 25.4 Viravapupphiyattheraapadāna

(14.)

3053\. _“Khīṇāsavasahassehi,_  
_niyyāti lokanāyako;_  
_Viravapupphamādāya,_  
_buddhassa abhiropayiṃ._  

(15.)

3054\. _Ekanavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

(16.)

3055\. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

3056\. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā viravapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
