# 32.10 Kumudamāliyattheraapadāna

(54.)

3577\. _“Usabhaṃ pavaraṃ vīraṃ,_  
_mahesiṃ vijitāvinaṃ;_  
_Vipassinaṃ mahāvīraṃ,_  
_abhijātaṃva kesariṃ._  

(55.)

3578\. _Rathiyaṃ paṭipajjantaṃ,_  
_āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ;_  
_Gahetvā kumudaṃ mālaṃ,_  
_buddhaseṭṭhaṃ samokiriṃ._  

(56.)

3579\. _Ekanavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

(57.)
