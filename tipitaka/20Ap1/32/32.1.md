

# 32.1 Ārakkhadāyakattheraapadāna



(1.)

3506\. _“Dhammadassissa munino,_  
_vati kārāpitā mayā;_  
_Ārakkho ca mayā dinno,_  
_dvipadindassa tādino._  


(2.)

3507\. _Aṭṭhārase kappasate,_  
_yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;_  
_Tena kammavisesena,_  
_patto me āsavakkhayo._  


(3.)

3508\. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;_  
_Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


3509\. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ārakkhadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

---

3510\. Ārakkhadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
