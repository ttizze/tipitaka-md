

# 21.1 Kaṇikārapupphiyattheraapadāna



(1.)

2750\. _“Kaṇikāraṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,_  
_ocinitvānahaṃ tadā;_  
_Tissassa abhiropesiṃ,_  
_oghatiṇṇassa tādino._  


(2.)

2751\. _Dvenavute ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(3.)

2752\. _Pañcattiṃse ito kappe,_  
_aruṇapāṇīti vissuto;_  
_Sattaratanasampanno,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  


(4.)

2753\. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;_  
_Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


2754\. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kaṇikārapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

---

2755\. Kaṇikārapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
