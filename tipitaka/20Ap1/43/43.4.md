# 43.4 Sattakadambapupphiyattheraapadāna

(61.)

5536\. _“Himavantassāvidūre,_  
_kukkuṭo nāma pabbato;_  
_Tamhi pabbatapādamhi,_  
_satta buddhā vasiṃsu te._  

(62.)

5537\. _Kadambaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,_  
_paggahetvāna añjaliṃ;_  
_Satta mālā gahetvāna,_  
_puññacittena okiriṃ._  

(63.)

5538\. _Tena kammena sukatena,_  
_cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;_  
_Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,_  
_tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ._  

(64.)

5539\. _Catunnavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

(65.)

5540\. _Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,_  
_…pe…_  
_viharāmi anāsavo._  

(66.)

5541\. _Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ._  

(67.)

5542\. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

5543\. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sattakadambapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

---

5544\. Sattakadambapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
