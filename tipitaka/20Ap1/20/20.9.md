

# 20.9 Kiṃkaṇikapupphiyattheraapadāna



(47.)

2728\. _“Sumaṅgaloti nāmena,_  
_sayambhū aparājito;_  
_Pavanā nikkhamitvāna,_  
_nagaraṃ pāvisī jino._  


(48.)

2729\. _Piṇḍacāraṃ caritvāna,_  
_nikkhamma nagarā muni;_  
_Katakiccova sambuddho,_  
_so vasī vanamantare._  


(49.)

2730\. _Kiṃkaṇipupphaṃ paggayha,_  
_buddhassa abhiropayiṃ;_  
_Pasannacitto sumano,_  
_sayambhussa mahesino._  


(50.)

2731\. _Catunnavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(51.)

2732\. _Chaḷāsītimhito kappe,_  
_apilāsisanāmako;_  
_Sattaratanasampanno,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  


(52.)

2733\. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


2734\. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kiṃkaṇikapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

---

2735\. Kiṃkaṇikapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
