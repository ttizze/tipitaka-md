

# 51.7 Ajeliphaladāyakattheraapadāna



(74.)

6653\. _“Ajjuno nāma sambuddho,_  
_himavante vasī tadā;_  
_Caraṇena ca sampanno,_  
_samādhikusalo muni._  


(75.)

6654\. _Kumbhamattaṃ gahetvāna,_  
_ajeliṃ jīvajīvakaṃ;_  
_Chattapaṇṇaṃ gahetvāna,_  
_adāsiṃ satthuno ahaṃ._  


(76.)

6655\. _Catunnavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(77.)

6656\. _Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,_  
_…pe…_  
_viharāmi anāsavo._  


(78.)

6657\. _Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ._  


(79.)

6658\. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._
