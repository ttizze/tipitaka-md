# 39.1 Avaṭaphaladāyakattheraapadāna

(1.)

4177\. _“Sataraṃsi nāma bhagavā,_  
_sayambhū aparājito;_  
_Vivekakāmo sambuddho,_  
_gocarāyābhinikkhami._  

(2.)

4178\. _Phalahattho ahaṃ disvā,_  
_upagacchiṃ narāsabhaṃ;_  
_Pasannacitto sumano,_  
_avaṭaṃ adadiṃ phalaṃ._  

(3.)

4179\. _Catunnavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ phalamadadiṃ ahaṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

(4.)

4180\. _Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,_  
_bhavā sabbe samūhatā;_  
_Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,_  
_viharāmi anāsavo._  

(5.)

4181\. _Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,_  
_mama buddhassa santike;_  
_Tisso vijjā anuppattā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ._  

(6.)

4182\. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;_  
_Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

4183\. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā avaṭaphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

---

4184\. Avaṭaphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
