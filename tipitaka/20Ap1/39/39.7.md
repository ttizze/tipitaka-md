# 39.7 Kadaliphaladāyakattheraapadāna

(37.)

4225\. _“Kaṇikāraṃva jalitaṃ,_  
_puṇṇamāyeva candimaṃ;_  
_Jalantaṃ dīparukkhaṃva,_  
_addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ._  

(38.)

4226\. _Kadaliphalaṃ paggayha,_  
_adāsiṃ satthuno ahaṃ;_  
_Pasannacitto sumano,_  
_vanditvāna apakkamiṃ._  

(39.)

4227\. _Ekattiṃse ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ phalamadadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

(40.)

4228\. _Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,_  
_…pe…_  
_viharāmi anāsavo._  

(41.)

4229\. _Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ._  

(42.)

4230\. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

4231\. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kadaliphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

---

4232\. Kadaliphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
