

# 53.5 Supaṭadāyakattheraapadāna



(31.)

6836\. _“Divāvihārā nikkhanto,_  
_vipassī lokanāyako;_  
_Lahuṃ supaṭakaṃ datvā,_  
_kappaṃ saggamhi modahaṃ._  


(32.)

6837\. _Ekanavutito kappe,_  
_supaṭakamadāsahaṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_supaṭassa idaṃ phalaṃ._  


(33.)

6838\. _Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,_  
_…pe…_  
_viharāmi anāsavo._  


(34.)

6839\. _Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ._  


(35.)

6840\. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


6841\. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā supaṭadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

---

6842\. Supaṭadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
