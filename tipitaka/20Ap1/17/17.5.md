

# 17.5 Kaṇikārachattiyattheraapadāna



(23.)

2463\. _“Vessabhū nāma sambuddho,_  
_lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;_  
_Divāvihārāya muni,_  
_ogāhayi mahāvanaṃ._  


(24.)

2464\. _Kaṇikāraṃ ocinitvā,_  
_chattaṃ katvānahaṃ tadā;_  
_Pupphacchadanaṃ katvāna,_  
_buddhassa abhiropayiṃ._  


(25.)

2465\. _Ekattiṃse ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  


(26.)

2466\. _Ito vīsatikappamhi,_  
_soṇṇābhā aṭṭha khattiyā;_  
_Sattaratanasampannā,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalā._  


(27.)

2467\. _Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_…pe…_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  


2468\. Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kaṇikārachattiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

---

2469\. Kaṇikārachattiyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
