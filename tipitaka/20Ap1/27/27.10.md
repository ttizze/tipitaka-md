# 27.10 Taraṇiyattheraapadāna

(39.)

3214\. _“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇo sambuddho,_  
_vipassī dakkhiṇāraho;_  
_Nadītīre ṭhito satthā,_  
_bhikkhusaṃghapurakkhato._  

(40.)

3215\. _Nāvā na vijjate tattha,_  
_santāraṇī mahaṇṇave;_  
_Nadiyā abhinikkhamma,_  
_tāresiṃ lokanāyakaṃ._  

(41.)

3216\. _Ekanavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ tāresiṃ naruttamaṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_taraṇāya idaṃ phalaṃ._  

(42.)

3221\. Tassuddānaṃ

3222\. _Ukkhipī telacandī ca,_  
_dīpado ca biḷālido;_  
_Maccho javo saḷalado,_  
_rakkhaso taraṇo dasa;_  
_Gāthāyo cettha saṅkhātā,_  
_tālīsaṃ cekameva cāti._
