# Āhārādi

(47.)

2564\. Maggārammaṇo dhammo maggārammaṇassa dhammassa āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo…  jhānapaccayena paccayo…  maggapaccayena paccayo…  sampayuttapaccayena paccayo…  atthipaccayena paccayo (ime satta paccayā sattarasa pañhā hetusadisā)…  natthipaccayena paccayo…  vigatapaccayena paccayo (anantarasadisā)…  avigatapaccayena paccayo. (Sattarasa pañhā.)
