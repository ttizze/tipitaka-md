(2.)

2762\. Ajjhattaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ajjhatto dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā—  ajjhattaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe paṭicca…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe ajjhattaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  vatthuṃ paṭicca khandhā. (1)

2763\. Bahiddhā dhammaṃ paṭicca bahiddhā dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā—  bahiddhā ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  vatthuṃ paṭicca khandhā. (1)
