# Naadhipati

(30.)

2326\. Micchattaniyataṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho micchattaniyato dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā—  micchattaniyate khandhe saṃsaṭṭho micchattaniyatādhipati. (1)

2327\. Sammattaniyataṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho sammattaniyato dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā—  sammattaniyate khandhe saṃsaṭṭho sammattaniyatādhipati. (1)
