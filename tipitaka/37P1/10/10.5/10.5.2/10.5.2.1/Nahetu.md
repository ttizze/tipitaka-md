# Nahetu

(70.)

1257\. Dassanena pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate khandhe saṃsaṭṭho vicikicchāsahagato moho. (1)

1258\. Bhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  uddhaccasahagate khandhe saṃsaṭṭho uddhaccasahagato moho. (1)
