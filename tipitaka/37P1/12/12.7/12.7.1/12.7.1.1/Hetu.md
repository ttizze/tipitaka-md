

# Hetu






(42.)

1771\. Sekkho dhammo sekkhassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo—  sekkhā hetū sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ hetupaccayena paccayo. (1)

1772\. Sekkho dhammo nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo—  sekkhā hetū cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ hetupaccayena paccayo. (2)

1773\. Sekkho dhammo sekkhassa ca nevasekkhanāsekkhassa ca dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo—  sekkhā hetū sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ hetupaccayena paccayo. (3)
