# 7.5.6 Satapattaṅgapañha

(270.)

1912\. “Bhante nāgasena, ‘satapattassa ekaṃ aṅgaṃ gahetabban’ti yaṃ vadesi, katamaṃ taṃ ekaṃ aṅgaṃ gahetabban”ti? “Yathā, mahārāja, satapatto ravitvā paresaṃ khemaṃ vā bhayaṃ vā ācikkhati; evameva kho, mahārāja, yoginā yogāvacarena paresaṃ dhammaṃ desayamānena vinipātaṃ bhayato dassayitabbaṃ, nibbānaṃ khemato dassayitabbaṃ. Idaṃ, mahārāja, satapattassa ekaṃ aṅgaṃ gahetabbaṃ. Bhāsitampetaṃ, mahārāja, therena piṇḍolabhāradvājena—

1913\. _‘Niraye bhayasantāsaṃ,_  
_nibbāne vipulaṃ sukhaṃ;_  
_Ubhayānetānatthāni,_  
_dassetabbāni yoginā’”ti._  

---

1914\. Satapattaṅgapañho chaṭṭho.
