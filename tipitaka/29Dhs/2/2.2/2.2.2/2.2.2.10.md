

# 2.2.2.10 Dasaka





(592.)

1228\. Dasavidhena rūpasaṅgaho—

1229\. Cakkhundriyaṃ, sotindriyaṃ, ghānindriyaṃ, jivhindriyaṃ kāyindriyaṃ, itthindriyaṃ, purisindriyaṃ, jīvitindriyaṃ, na indriyaṃ rūpaṃ atthi sappaṭighaṃ, atthi appaṭighaṃ.

---

1230\. Evaṃ dasavidhena rūpasaṅgaho.

  
Dasakaṃ.
