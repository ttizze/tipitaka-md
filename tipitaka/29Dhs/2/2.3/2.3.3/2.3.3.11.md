

# 2.3.3.11 Bhavadiṭṭhiduka





(1320.)

2025\. Tattha katamā bhavadiṭṭhi? Bhavissati attā ca loko cāti, yā evarūpā diṭṭhi diṭṭhigataṃ…pe…  vipariyāsaggāho—  ayaṃ vuccati bhavadiṭṭhi.

(1321.)

2026\. Tattha katamā vibhavadiṭṭhi? Na bhavissati attā ca loko cāti, yā evarūpā diṭṭhi diṭṭhigataṃ…pe…  vipariyāsaggāho—  ayaṃ vuccati vibhavadiṭṭhi.
