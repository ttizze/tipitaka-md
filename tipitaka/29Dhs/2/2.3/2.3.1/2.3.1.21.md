# 2.3.1.21 Ajjhattārammaṇattika

(1053.)

1718\. Katame dhammā ajjhattārammaṇā? Ajjhatte dhamme ārabbha ye uppajjanti cittacetasikā dhammā—  ime dhammā ajjhattārammaṇā.

(1054.)

1719\. Katame dhammā bahiddhārammaṇā? Bahiddhā dhamme ārabbha ye uppajjanti cittacetasikā dhammā—  ime dhammā bahiddhārammaṇā.

(1055.)

1720\. Katame dhammā ajjhattabahiddhārammaṇā? Ajjhattabahiddhā dhamme ārabbha ye uppajjanti cittacetasikā dhammā—  ime dhammā ajjhattabahiddhārammaṇā.
