# 2.1.2.6 Aṭṭhakasiṇaṃ soḷasakkhattukaṃ

## 624\. Aṭṭhakasiṇaṃ soḷasakkhattukaṃ

(203.)

625\. Katame dhammā kusalā? Yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati āpokasiṇaṃ…pe…  tejokasiṇaṃ…pe…  vāyokasiṇaṃ…pe…  nīlakasiṇaṃ…pe…  pītakasiṇaṃ…pe…  lohitakasiṇaṃ…pe…  odātakasiṇaṃ, tasmiṃ samaye phasso hoti…pe…  avikkhepo hoti…pe…  ime dhammā kusalā.
