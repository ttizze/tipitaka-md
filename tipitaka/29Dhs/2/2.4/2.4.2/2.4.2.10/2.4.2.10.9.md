

# 2.4.2.10.9 Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaduka






(1541.)

2252\. Katame dhammā cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānā? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  ime dhammā cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānā.

(1542.)

2253\. Katame dhammā no cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānā? Cittañca, rūpañca, nibbānañca—  ime dhammā no cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānā.
