

# 2.1.8 Surādhattheragāthā




(135.)

295\. _“Khīṇā hi mayhaṃ jāti,_  
_vusitaṃ jinasāsanaṃ;_  
_Pahīno jālasaṅkhāto,_  
_bhavanetti samūhatā._  


(136.)

296\. _Yassatthāya pabbajito,_  
_agārasmānagāriyaṃ;_  
_So me attho anuppatto,_  
_sabbasaṃyojanakkhayo”ti._
