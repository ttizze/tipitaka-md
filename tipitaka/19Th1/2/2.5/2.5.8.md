# 2.5.8 Vajjitattheragāthā

(215.)

423\. _“Saṃsaraṃ dīghamaddhānaṃ,_  
_gatīsu parivattisaṃ;_  
_Apassaṃ ariyasaccāni,_  
_andhabhūto puthujjano._

_natthi dāni punabbhavo”ti._
