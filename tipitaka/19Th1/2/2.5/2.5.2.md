

# 2.5.2 Dhammapālattheragāthā




(203.)

405\. _“Yo have daharo bhikkhu,_  
_yuñjati buddhasāsane;_  
_Jāgaro sa hi suttesu,_  
_amoghaṃ tassa jīvitaṃ._  


(204.)

406\. _Tasmā saddhañca sīlañca,_  
_pasādaṃ dhammadassanaṃ;_  
_Anuyuñjetha medhāvī,_  
_saraṃ buddhāna sāsanan”ti._
