# 2.4.8 Nisabhattheragāthā

(195.)

391\. _“Pañca kāmaguṇe hitvā,_  
_Piyarūpe manorame;_  
_Saddhāya gharā nikkhamma,_  
_Dukkhassantakaro bhave._
