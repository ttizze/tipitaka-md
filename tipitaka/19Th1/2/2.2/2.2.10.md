

# 2.2.10 Sirimattheragāthā




(159.)

333\. _“Pare ca naṃ pasaṃsanti,_  
_attā ce asamāhito;_  
_Moghaṃ pare pasaṃsanti,_  
_attā hi asamāhito._  


(160.)

334\. _Pare ca naṃ garahanti,_  
_attā ce susamāhito;_  
_Moghaṃ pare garahanti,_  
_attā hi susamāhito”ti._

336\. Tassuddānaṃ



337\. _Cundo ca jotidāso ca,_  
_thero heraññakāni ca;_  
_Somamitto sabbamitto,_  
_kāḷo tisso ca kimilo;_  
_Nando ca sirimā ceva,_  
_dasa therā mahiddhikāti._
