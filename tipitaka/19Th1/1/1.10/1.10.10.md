# 1.10.10 (Dutiya) Devasabhattheragāthā

(100.)

223\. _“Sammappadhānasampanno,_  
_Satipaṭṭhānagocaro;_  
_Vimuttikusumasañchanno,_  
_Parinibbissatyanāsavo”ti._  

---

224\. …  Devasabho thero… .

Vaggo dasamo.

---

225\. Tassuddānaṃ

226\. _Paripuṇṇako ca vijayo,_  
_erako mettajī muni;_  
_Cakkhupālo khaṇḍasumano,_  
_tisso ca abhayo tathā;_  
_Uttiyo ca mahāpañño,_  
_thero devasabhopi cāti._
