

# 1.6.10 Sīvalittheragāthā




(60.)

135\. _“Te me ijjhiṃsu saṅkappā,_  
_yadattho pāvisiṃ kuṭiṃ;_  
_Vijjāvimuttiṃ paccesaṃ,_  
_mānānusayamujjahan”ti._

137\. Tassuddānaṃ



138\. _Godhiko ca subāhu ca,_  
_valliyo uttiyo isi;_  
_Añjanavaniyo thero,_  
_duve kuṭivihārino;_  
_Ramaṇīyakuṭiko ca,_  
_kosalavhayasīvalīti._
