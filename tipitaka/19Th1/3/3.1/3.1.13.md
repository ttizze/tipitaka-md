# 3.1.13 Abhibhūtattheragāthā

(255.)

482\. _“Suṇātha ñātayo sabbe,_  
_yāvantettha samāgatā;_  
_Dhammaṃ vo desayissāmi,_  
_dukkhā jāti punappunaṃ._  

(256.)

483\. _Ārambhatha nikkamatha,_  
_Yuñjatha buddhasāsane;_  
_Dhunātha maccuno senaṃ,_  
_Naḷāgāraṃva kuñjaro._  

(257.)

484\. _Yo imasmiṃ dhammavinaye,_  
_appamatto vihassati;_  
_Pahāya jātisaṃsāraṃ,_  
_dukkhassantaṃ karissatī”ti._
