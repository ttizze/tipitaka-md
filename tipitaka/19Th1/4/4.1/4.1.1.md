# 4.1.1 Nāgasamālattheragāthā

(267.)

503\. _“Alaṅkatā suvasanā,_  
_mālinī candanussadā;_  
_Majjhe mahāpathe nārī,_  
_tūriye naccati naṭṭakī._  

(268.)

504\. _Piṇḍikāya paviṭṭhohaṃ,_  
_gacchanto naṃ udikkhisaṃ;_  
_Alaṅkataṃ suvasanaṃ,_  
_maccupāsaṃva oḍḍitaṃ._  

(269.)

505\. _Tato me manasīkāro,_  
_yoniso udapajjatha;_  
_Ādīnavo pāturahu,_  
_nibbidā samatiṭṭhatha._  

(270.)

506\. _Tato cittaṃ vimucci me,_  
_passa dhammasudhammataṃ;_  
_Tisso vijjā anuppattā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanan”ti._
