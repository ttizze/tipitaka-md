

# 23 Upādinnattika, Kusalattika


(40.)

108\. Anupādinnupādāniyaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naupādinnupādāniyo nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.

109\. Anupādinnaanupādāniyaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanupādinnaanupādāniyo nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  avigate cha.

110\. Anupādinnupādāniyaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naupādinnupādāniyo naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.

111\. Upādinnupādāniyaṃ abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paccayā naupādinnupādāniyo naabyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca…pe…  avigate pañca.
