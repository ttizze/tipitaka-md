# 4.1.16--20

(184.)

279\. “Dvīhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato sukhaṃ viharati. Katamehi dvīhi? Akkodhena ca anupanāhena ca…  amakkhena ca apaḷāsena ca…  anissāya ca amacchariyena ca…  amāyāya ca asāṭheyyena ca…  hiriyā ca ottappena ca. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, dvīhi dhammehi samannāgato sukhaṃ viharati”.
