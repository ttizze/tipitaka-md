# 13.2.1 Kusala

(683.)

1990\. Catasso appamaññāyo—  mettā, karuṇā, muditā, upekkhā.

(684.)

1991\. Tattha katamā mettā? Idha bhikkhu yasmiṃ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṃ bhāveti vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharati mettāsahagataṃ, yā tasmiṃ samaye metti mettāyanā mettāyitattaṃ mettācetovimutti—  ayaṃ vuccati “mettā”. Avasesā dhammā mettāya sampayuttā. (1:*1*)
