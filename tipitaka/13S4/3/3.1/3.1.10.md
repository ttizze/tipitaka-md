# 3.1.10 Dussīlasutta

(289.)

1259\. “Pañcahi, anuruddha, dhammehi samannāgato mātugāmo…pe…  nirayaṃ upapajjati. Katamehi pañcahi? Assaddho ca hoti, ahiriko ca hoti, anottappī ca hoti, dussīlo ca hoti, duppañño ca hoti—  imehi kho, anuruddha, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato mātugāmo…pe…  nirayaṃ upapajjatī”ti.

---

1260\. Dasamaṃ.
