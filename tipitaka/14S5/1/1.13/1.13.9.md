

# 1.13.9 Vedanāsutta




(169.)

326\. “Tisso imā, bhikkhave, vedanā. Katamā tisso? Sukhā vedanā, dukkhā vedanā, adukkhamasukhā vedanā—  imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso vedanā. Imāsaṃ kho, bhikkhave, tissannaṃ vedanānaṃ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya…pe…  ayaṃ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo”ti.
