# 1.13.7 Malasutta

(167.)

322\. “Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, malāni. Katamāni tīṇi? Rāgo malaṃ, doso malaṃ, moho malaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi malāni. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, tiṇṇannaṃ malānaṃ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya…pe…  ayaṃ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo”ti.
