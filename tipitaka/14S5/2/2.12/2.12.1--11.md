

# 2.12.1--11 Esanādisutta




(292--301.)

714\. “Tisso imā, bhikkhave, esanā. Katamā tisso? Kāmesanā, bhavesanā, brahmacariyesanā”ti vitthāretabbaṃ.

---

715\. Esanāvaggo dvādasamo.



---

716\. Tassuddānaṃ



717\. _Esanā vidhā āsavo,_  
_Bhavo ca dukkhatā tisso;_  
_Khilaṃ malañca nīgho ca,_  
_Vedanā taṇhā tasināya cāti._  


718\. (Bojjhaṅgasaṃyuttassa esanāpeyyālaṃ vivekanissitato vitthāretabbaṃ.)
