

# 7.3.4 Suddhikasutta




(836.)

1481\. “Cattārome, bhikkhave, iddhipādā. Katame cattāro? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu chandasamādhippadhānasaṅkhārasamannāgataṃ iddhipādaṃ bhāveti, vīriyasamādhi…pe…  cittasamādhi…  vīmaṃsāsamādhippadhānasaṅkhārasamannāgataṃ iddhipādaṃ bhāveti—  ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro iddhipādā”ti.
