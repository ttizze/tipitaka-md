# 6.9.1--11 Punaesanādisutta

(792--802.)

1331\. (Evaṃ esanāpāḷi vitthāretabbā—  rāgavinayapariyosānaṃ dosavinayapariyosānaṃ mohavinayapariyosānaṃ.)

---

1332\. Esanāvaggo navamo.

---

1333\. Tassuddānaṃ

1334\. _Esanā vidhā āsavo,_  
_Bhavo ca dukkhatā tisso;_  
_Khilaṃ malañca nīgho ca,_  
_Vedanā taṇhā tasinā cāti._
