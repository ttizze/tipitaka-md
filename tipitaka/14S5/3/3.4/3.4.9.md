# 3.4.9 Bhāvanāsutta

(405.)

930\. “Catunnaṃ, bhikkhave, satipaṭṭhānānaṃ bhāvanaṃ desessāmi. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamā, bhikkhave, catunnaṃ satipaṭṭhānānaṃ bhāvanā? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu kāye kāyānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhijjhādomanassaṃ; vedanāsu…pe…  citte…pe…  dhammesu dhammānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhijjhādomanassaṃ. Ayaṃ kho, bhikkhave, catunnaṃ satipaṭṭhānānaṃ bhāvanā”ti.
