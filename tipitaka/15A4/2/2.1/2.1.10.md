# 2.1.10 Gihisāmīcisutta

(60.)

431\. Atha kho anāthapiṇḍiko gahapati yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ nisīdi. Ekamantaṃ nisinnaṃ kho anāthapiṇḍikaṃ gahapatiṃ bhagavā etadavoca—

432\. “Catūhi kho, gahapati, dhammehi samannāgato ariyasāvako gihisāmīcipaṭipadaṃ paṭipanno hoti yasopaṭilābhiniṃ saggasaṃvattanikaṃ. Katamehi catūhi? Idha, gahapati, ariyasāvako bhikkhusaṃghaṃ paccupaṭṭhito hoti cīvarena, bhikkhusaṃghaṃ paccupaṭṭhito hoti piṇḍapātena, bhikkhusaṃghaṃ paccupaṭṭhito hoti senāsanena, bhikkhusaṃghaṃ paccupaṭṭhito hoti gilānappaccayabhesajjaparikkhārena. Imehi kho, gahapati, catūhi dhammehi samannāgato ariyasāvako gihisāmīcipaṭipadaṃ paṭipanno hoti yasopaṭilābhiniṃ saggasaṃvattanikanti.

433\. _Gihisāmīcipaṭipadaṃ,_  
_paṭipajjanti paṇḍitā;_  
_Sammaggate sīlavante,_  
_cīvarena upaṭṭhitā._  

434\. _Piṇḍapātasayanena,_  
_Gilānappaccayena ca;_  
_Tesaṃ divā ca ratto ca,_  
_Sadā puññaṃ pavaḍḍhati;_  
_Saggañca kamatiṭṭhānaṃ,_  
_Kammaṃ katvāna bhaddakan”ti._
