

# 5.1.6 Saññāsutta




(317.)

1079\. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Yo kho, bhikkhave, rūpasaññāya uppādo ṭhiti…pe…  jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo…pe…  yo dhammasaññāya uppādo ṭhiti abhinibbatti pātubhāvo, dukkhasseso uppādo, rogānaṃ ṭhiti, jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo. Yo ca kho, bhikkhave, rūpasaññāya nirodho…pe…  jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo…pe…  yo dhammasaññāya nirodho vūpasamo atthaṅgamo, dukkhasseso nirodho, rogānaṃ vūpasamo, jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo”ti.
