

# 1.2.3 Natthiputtasamasutta




(13.)

46\. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—

47\. _“Natthi puttasamaṃ pemaṃ,_  
_natthi gosamitaṃ dhanaṃ;_  
_Natthi sūriyasamā ābhā,_  
_samuddaparamā sarā”ti._  


48\. _“Natthi attasamaṃ pemaṃ,_  
_natthi dhaññasamaṃ dhanaṃ;_  
_Natthi paññāsamā ābhā,_  
_vuṭṭhi ve paramā sarā”ti._
