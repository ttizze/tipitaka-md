

# 1.5.1 Ādittasutta




(41.)

238\. Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Atha kho aññatarā devatā abhikkantāya rattiyā abhikkantavaṇṇā kevalakappaṃ jetavanaṃ obhāsetvā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ aṭṭhāsi. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavato santike imā gāthāyo abhāsi—

239\. _“Ādittasmiṃ agārasmiṃ,_  
_yaṃ nīharati bhājanaṃ;_  
_Taṃ tassa hoti atthāya,_  
_no ca yaṃ tattha ḍayhati._  


240\. _Evaṃ ādittako loko,_  
_jarāya maraṇena ca;_  
_Nīharetheva dānena,_  
_dinnaṃ hoti sunīhataṃ._  


241\. _Dinnaṃ sukhaphalaṃ hoti,_  
_nādinnaṃ hoti taṃ tathā;_  
_Corā haranti rājāno,_  
_aggi ḍahati nassati._  


242\. _Atha antena jahati,_  
_Sarīraṃ sapariggahaṃ;_  
_Etadaññāya medhāvī,_  
_Bhuñjetha ca dadetha ca;_  
_Datvā ca bhutvā ca yathānubhāvaṃ,_  
_Anindito saggamupeti ṭhānan”ti._
