

# 1.28 Hetuduka, Cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaduka



(52.)

150\. Hetuṃ cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhāno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Sabbattha nava.)

151\. Nahetuṃ nocittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu nocittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhāno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Sabbattha ekaṃ. Sabbattha vitthāro.)
